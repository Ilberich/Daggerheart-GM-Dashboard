GRAPPLE ACTION ›

Make an Agility roll against the target's Agility DC. On a success, the target gains the Restrained condition. While Restrained, they cannot move and have a −2 to all rolls. They may spend their action to attempt to escape (Agility vs your Agility DC).

PRONE / STAND UP ACTION ›

A creature knocked prone is at −2 to attack rolls. Attacks made against them from adjacent squares gain +2. Standing up costs their full movement for that turn — they cannot also move.

VULNERABLE CONDITION ›

All damage taken is increased by the severity threshold. When a Vulnerable creature takes damage, treat the result as one severity level higher (Minor → Severe, Severe → Critical).

SPEND FEAR (GM MOVES) GM MOVE ›

Spend Fear tokens to activate adversary abilities marked with ✦. Each spend costs 1 Fear unless noted. Fear accrues when players roll with Fear, or from environment triggers. Typical GM moves: Escalate (bring in reinforcements), Reveal (spring a trap), Strike (adversary attacks out of turn).

HIDDEN CONDITION ›

A Hidden creature cannot be targeted by attacks. On their turn, they may attack — doing so reveals them. They can remain hidden if they succeed on a Stealth roll (DC set by enemy Instinct).

DISENGAGE (HOUSE RULE) CUSTOM ›

Spend your action to disengage. Until the start of your next turn, movement does not trigger opportunity attacks. (Added by GM — not in base rulebook.)
